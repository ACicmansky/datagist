# Product Context

## User Experience Goal
The application must feel "invisible" after setup.
1. **Login:** User signs in with Google.
2. **Connect:** User grants permission to read Analytics data.
3. **Set & Forget:** User chooses a plan and frequency.
4. **Value:** User receives a high-quality email report automatically.

## Pricing & Tiers
- **Free Plan:**
  - Frequency: Every 30 days.
  - Analysis: Standard summary of internal metrics.
  - Limits: 1 Website.
- **Pro Plan:**
  - Frequency: Custom (7 to 30 days).
  - Analysis: Detailed breakdown + Recommendations.
  - **Key Feature:** Gemini Grounding (AI searches web for external context/trends).
  - Complexity Slider: Brief vs. Detailed.
  - Limits: 1 Website.
- **Max Plan:**
  - All Pro features.
  - Limits: Up to 5 Websites.
  - Priority processing.

## AI Persona
The AI should act as a "Senior Data Consultant."
- **Tone:** Professional, objective, yet encouraging.
- **Output:** Clear headers, bullet points for metrics, and distinct "Strategic Recommendations" sections.
